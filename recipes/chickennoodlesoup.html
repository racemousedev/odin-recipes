Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This is a recipe for a chicken noodle soup made with homemade chicken stock, for stock recipe, click here.

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Leeks
  • Parsnips
  • Turnips
  • Garlic (entire head)
  • White onions
  • Chicken spines or wings
  • Vegetable or canola oil
  • Fresh thyme
  • Fresh parsley
  • Whole peppercorns
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh ginger
  • Fresh lemongrass
  • Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Fresh dill
  • Scallions
  • Salt
  • Egg noodles

Instructions

  1. Begin by roughly chopping celery, carrots, a finely chopped parsnip and turnip and palce into a bowl.
  2. Seperately chop an onion and set to the side.
  3. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium-high heat until shimmering then add the onions. Saute until softened.
  4. Add the rest of the vegetables and warming through before addin stock.
  5. Over high heat bring to a simmer.
  6. While coming up to a simmer, add in chopped parsley, grated ginger and lemongrass.
  7. Once boiling, add in the chicken thighs and skim off any fat that rises to the surface as it cooks.
  8. After 15-20 minutes, remove the chicken and shred using a pair of forks before adding back to the pot.
  9. Right at the end add fresh chopped dill along with minced scallions. Cook for no mroe than 1 minute. Season with salt.
  10. In a medium pot, add a few ladles full of the soup along with some noodles season with salt and cook until noodles are finished.
